Types of Surfboards

Funboards are good boards for beginners who want the maneuverability of a shortboard and the paddling power of a longboard.

Soft top are the best boards for first time surfers, soft, stable and most importantly, safe!

Shortboards perks are their speed and maneuverability while their drawbacks are stability and paddling power.

Hybrid boards combine stability with maneuverability. A nice option for intermediate level surfers.
